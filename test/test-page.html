The Margin Notes Test Page

This page exists to verify the Margin Notes extension. Open the sidebar with Alt+M, then click + Note and place notes around the page to confirm they anchor, persist, and print correctly.

Anchoring

Notes should anchor to the nearest DOM element via XPath. If the page is refreshed, every note should reappear in the same place. Try attaching a note to this bold word and refreshing.

"We know what we are, but know not what we may be." — Hamlet, Act IV, Scene V.

Persistence

Notes are stored in chrome.storage.local, keyed by origin + pathname. That means notes here live separately from notes on /test-page-2.html.
